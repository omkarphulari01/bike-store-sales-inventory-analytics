# 🚴 Bike Store Sales, Inventory & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes sales performance, inventory management, customer behavior, and staff performance for a bike retail business.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Objectives

- Track overall sales performance
- Analyze product and category revenue
- Monitor inventory levels
- Identify top customers
- Evaluate staff performance
- Analyze store-wise performance
- Track order trends over time

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- SQL Server
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX
- Power Query

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Sales Dashboard
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Brand
- Revenue by Product
- Revenue Trend Analysis

### Inventory Dashboard
- Stock by Store
- Stock by Brand
- Stock by Category
- Inventory Status Monitoring

### Customer & Staff Dashboard
- Top Customers
- Staff Revenue Analysis
- Monthly Orders
- Geographic Customer Insights

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue exceeded 8.5M.
- More than 1,400 customers generated sales.
- Mountain Bikes contributed the highest revenue.
- Baldwin Bikes generated the highest store revenue.
- Trek was the leading brand.
- Inventory levels remained sufficient across most stores.
